About Us

Digital Art Museum is a web project made by using Crust app and Polygon.

Digital art museum is an initiative to empower digital artists and photographers, usually when an art or a photograph is sold, the owner gets an one time payment. But it is very difficult for a small digital artist to sell their art.
Digital art museum gives all of these artists a platform to earn royalties based on the number of downloads their arts get, just like how a museum earns by displaying art peices, but this time the payment is given directly to the artists.
